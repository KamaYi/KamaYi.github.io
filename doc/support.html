Frequently Asked Questions

1 How do I create a time capsule?

Tap the "+" button, add your content, set an unlock date, and tap "Seal Capsule".

2 Can I open a capsule before the unlock date?

No, sealed capsules cannot be opened early. This preserves the surprise!

3 What happens when my capsule unlocks?

You'll receive a notification. Tap it to view your capsule's contents.

üîí Is my data secure?

Yes! Your data is encrypted during transmission (TLS/SSL) and stored securely on cloud servers with encryption. Only you can access your content through your authenticated account.

5 How long will my data be stored?

Your capsule content is stored until the capsule unlocks and you delete it, or until you delete your account. We retain your data as long as you need it.
